# Cactus Watch Status — 2026-04-06

## Session: SOSAZ Data Quality Audit

Compared Cactus Watch bill status data against Save Our Schools AZ (SOSAZ) tracked bills.
Found 37 of 46 SOSAZ bills were incorrectly marked dead in our system, plus 5 missing entirely.

## Bugs Found and Fixed

### Bug 1: Crossover deadline checker too aggressive (37 bills)

**Root cause:** Two SQL queries in `deadline-checker.js` were marking bills dead even when they had activity in the crossover chamber:

- **Section 2 (passed_committee):** Killed ALL `passed_committee` bills after the crossover deadline, regardless of whether they had crossover-chamber committee actions or floor actions.
- **Section 2c (crossover stalls):** Only checked for `cow`/`3rd_read` floor actions in the opposite chamber. Bills with 1st/2nd readings or committee hearings in the crossover chamber were incorrectly killed.

**Fix:** Both queries now include `NOT EXISTS` checks for both `committee_actions` AND `floor_actions` in the opposite chamber. Any crossover activity protects the bill from being marked dead.

**Files:** `tools/bill-tracker/workers/central/src/deadline-checker.js`

### Bug 2: HB4000+ bills not scraped (5 bills)

**Root cause:** Arizona Legislature numbers House bills with a gap (HB2999 → HB4001, nothing in between). The scraper's MISS_THRESHOLD of 10 consecutive 404s stops enumeration at HB3009, never reaching HB4001+.

**Fix:** Added `EXTRA_RANGES` array in `scraper.js` with `{ prefix: 'HB', body: 'H', start: 4001 }`. Both the cron handler and `runFullScrape` now iterate extra ranges after the main prefix scan.

**Files:** `tools/bill-tracker/workers/central/src/scraper.js`, `tools/bill-tracker/workers/central/src/index.js`

### Bug 3: Committee actions stored with wrong chamber

**Root cause:** `processCommitteeActions()` used `a.LegislativeBody` for the chamber, which is always null in azleg's API. Fell back to the bill's origin chamber. All committee actions (including crossover-chamber ones) were stored as the origin chamber.

**Fix:** Now uses `a.Committee.LegislativeBody` first, which IS populated by azleg. Falls back to `a.LegislativeBody` then origin chamber.

**Files:** `tools/bill-tracker/workers/central/src/scraper.js`
